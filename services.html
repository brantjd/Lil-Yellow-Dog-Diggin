Services

Practical dirt work, land cleanup, and Archie-approved improvements.

Useful work for small to mid-size properties, with enough personality to remind you this is not a giant corporate dirt robot.

Mini Excavator Work

Small trenching, drainage corrections, culvert prep, grading, stump area cleanup, and tight-access excavation. Archie calls it digging with paperwork.

Mowing

Pasture edges, lots, trails, and seasonal overgrowth control for properties that need more than lawn care.

Brush Clearing

Clearing saplings, heavy weeds, and tangled growth around access lanes, fields, and rough edges. Excellent for revealing things you forgot were there.

Fence Row Cleanup

Cutting back overgrowth and opening working space along existing or planned fence lines. Archie prefers boundaries he can understand.

Driveway Leveling

Grading ruts, shaping gravel, filling low spots, and improving water movement across rural drives. Your suspension sends thanks.

Property Cleanup

Custom cleanup work for neglected corners, trails, building sites, field entrances, and small acreage projects.

Simple project flow

  1. Walk the property. Review the access, slope, soil, drainage, and cleanup goals.
  2. Set the scope. Decide what gets cleared, moved, leveled, hauled, or left in place. Archie may sniff the plan.
  3. Get it usable. Complete the work with compact equipment sized for the job.
