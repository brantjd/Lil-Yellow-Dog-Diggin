Equipment

Compact equipment for tight access and practical results.

Compact equipment makes it possible to handle useful land work in tighter spaces without bringing in larger machinery than the job requires.

Mini excavator

10,000-pound machine with a 4-way adjustable blade — enough muscle for real dirt work, compact enough to reach tight access points without tearing up the property. The 4-way blade angles and tilts for precise grading, backfilling, and crowning.

Used for drainage work, grading, light digging, culvert prep, shoreline cleanup, and other tight-access property projects.

Mowing equipment

Used for rough mowing, growth control, and reclaiming areas that need regular maintenance or seasonal cleanup.

Grading tools

Used for shaping gravel, smoothing ruts, and improving driveway surface and water movement.
